MAG Screen Magnifier

MAG is a lightweight, high-performance screen magnifier for Windows. It renders a real-time zoomed view of the area around your cursor using OpenGL, with sub-frame latency and support for high-DPI and multi-monitor setups.

Getting Started

When MAG launches, a resizable magnifier window appears on your desktop. The window shows an enlarged view of whatever is beneath it.

  • Scroll the mouse wheel over the window to zoom in or out.
  • Drag the window by its frame to position it anywhere on screen.
  • Right-click the window frame to open the context menu.
Tip: Enable Follow Mouse from the context menu to lock the magnifier to your cursor. The window becomes click-through while tracking is active.
